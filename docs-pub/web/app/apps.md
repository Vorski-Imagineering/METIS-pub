# Apps

METIS is made of several independent pieces of functionality, each called an **app**. The
shared core — people, organisations, and the holons that represent them — is always there;
on top of it, each app adds its own features.

The current apps are:

- **Coherence** — conversations and the IRIS publishing pipeline.
- **The Gathering** — camps and local gatherings.
- **Audax** — quests and missions.
- **Outreach** — LinkedIn outreach.

Apps are independent: they can be worked on and used separately, and you don't need one to
use another.

## Different holons use different apps

Every holon belongs to one app, which decides what that holon can do and what appears on its
page — a camp behaves differently from a conversation because they belong to different apps.
This is why the same core CRM can host very different kinds of records.

## What you see

Two things decide whether an app shows up for you:

- **Access** — some apps require permission to use; if you don't have it, the app's
  navigation won't appear. See [Access & permissions](../../core/access-and-permissions.md).
- **Focus** — an app's navigation appears only when your current [Focus](focus-and-scoping.md)
  actually has records for that app.

So an app can be hidden either because you lack access or because your current Focus has none
of its records — see the two guides above to tell the difference.
