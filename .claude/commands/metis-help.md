# METIS Help

Then follow up with a section of **suggested queries** the user can type right now, tailored to what METIS tracks. Present them as ready-to-run examples:

## What you can ask with `/metis`

**Your worklist**
- `/metis who should I contact today?`
- `/metis show me my overdue items`
- `/metis what's due this week?`

**People**
- `/metis find person named "Alice"`
- `/metis show me the profile for [name]`
- `/metis who is responsible for [name]?`

**Organisations & holons**
- `/metis get holon by slug borderland-2026`
- `/metis list all organisations in the PT 2026 Orgs journey`
- `/metis show members of the [holon name] holon`

**Journey & pipeline**
- `/metis show all contacts in the "Interested in joining" step`
- `/metis which orgs are in pre-confirmation talks?`

**Recording updates**
- `/metis add note to relationship 42: "Had a great call, following up next week"`
- `/metis advance relationship 42 to the next step and add a note`
